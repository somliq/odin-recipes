Lasagna

Description

This is a rich, cheesy, and hearty lasagna recipe perfect for a family dinner or a special gathering. It features layers of fresh pasta, seasoned meat sauce, and a creamy ricotta and mozzarella cheese blend. It takes a little effort, but the results are always worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Eme kanan
  2. Cook the ground beef until brown, then drain excess fat. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese and egg.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Assemble the lasagna by layering meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Cover the dish and bake for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for 15 more minutes until the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
